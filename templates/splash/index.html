What's it all about?

We've noticed that finding a good product or excellent services is like finding a needle in a haystack. You to search the web, read blogs, ask friends, scour directories and then call everywhere.

It's also very difficult to sell these days, there's just so much noise out there. There are many companies out there looking for solutions that exist but that they can't find.

We think this is all a very inefficient process of matching supply and demand. With unprob, more matches will be made, more problems will be solved and businesses all over the world will run more smoothly.

We use Machine Learning and Web Crawling to automatically bring you the best results and prices for any problem you have. We still use our own brains a lot as well to ensure good results.
